# Related Work on Imitation Learning

## Behavioral Cloning
- Pomerleau, D. A. (1989). ALVINN: An autonomous land vehicle in a neural network
  - Foundational imitation learning work
  - Recognition of distribution shift problem

## Dataset Aggregation (DAgger)
- Ross, D., Gordon, G., & Bagnell, J. D. (2011). A reduction of imitation learning and structured prediction to no-regret online learning
  - DAgger algorithm
  - Addresses distribution shift online

## Inverse Reinforcement Learning
- Russell, S. J. (1998). Learning agents for uncertain environments
  - IRL formulation
  - Reward learning from demonstrations

- Abbeel, P., & Ng, A. Y. (2004). Apprenticeship learning via inverse reinforcement learning
  - Maximum entropy IRL
  - Feature-based reward learning

## GAIL (Generative Adversarial Imitation Learning)
- Ho, D., & Ermon, S. (2016). Generative adversarial imitation learning
  - Discriminator-based imitation
  - Connection to maximum-entropy IRL

## Generative Models for Imitation
- Rajeswaran, A. et al. (2017). Learning complex dexterous manipulation with deep reinforcement learning and demonstrations
  - Deep IL from demonstrations
  - Combination with RL fine-tuning

## Recent Work: Adversarial Domain Adaptation
- Tzeng et al. (2017). Simultaneous deep transfer across domains and tasks
  - Domain adaptation for IL
  - Discriminator-based adaptation
