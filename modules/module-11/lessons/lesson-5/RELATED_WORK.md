# Related Work on Meta-Learning and Adaptation

## Meta-Learning Surveys
- Hospedales et al. (2020). Meta-learning in neural networks: A survey
  - Comprehensive meta-learning overview
  - Taxonomy of methods

## Model-Agnostic Meta-Learning (MAML)
- Finn, C., Abbeel, P., & Levine, S. (2017). Model-agnostic meta-learning for fast adaptation of deep networks
  - MAML algorithm
  - Gradient-based meta-learning
  - Few-shot learning foundations

## Task-Conditioned Adaptation
- Jaderberg et al. (2016). Spatial transformer networks
  - Learnable task adaptation
  - Feature transformation

## Hyperparameter Meta-Learning
- Vanschoren, J. (2018). Meta-learning: A survey
  - Learning hyperparameters via meta-learning
  - Auto-tuning algorithms

## Contextual Meta-Learning
- Rabinowitz et al. (2019). Machine theory of mind
  - Context-dependent task adaptation
  - Theory of mind in RL
