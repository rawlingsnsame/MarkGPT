# Lesson 10: Summary

Production RL requires fundamentally different approaches than research RL. Safety, stability, sample efficiency, and continuous improvement are paramount. Offline learning enables decisions without affecting systems. Exploration must be managed carefully. Continuous monitoring and retraining keep systems competitive. Real-world applications demonstrate that combining RL with engineering produces better results than pure RL.

**Core Learning Outcomes:**
- Production RL prioritizes safety and stability over optimality
- Offline learning enables RL without live environment interaction
- Exploration budgets limit the cost of learning in deployment
- Continuous monitoring enables rapid problem detection
- Gradual deployment (canary, A/B testing) manages risk
- Combining RL with rules, planning, and classical methods works best
- Successful production systems emphasize infrastructure and engineering

For practitioners: Start simple, validate improvements, instrument systems thoroughly, prioritize safety from day one.
