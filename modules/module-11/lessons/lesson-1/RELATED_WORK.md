# Related Work on Reinforcement Learning Fundamentals

## Foundational Papers
- Sutton, R. S. (1988). Learning to predict by the methods of temporal differences. 
  - Introduced temporal difference learning, cornerstone of modern RL

- Konda, V. R., & Tsitsiklis, J. N. (2000). Actor-critic algorithms.
  - Unified framework for policy gradient and value function methods

## Solution Methods
- Watkins, C. J. C. H., & Dayan, P. (1992). Q-learning.
  - Off-policy learning method; forms basis of DQN

## Recent Extensions
- Nachum, O., Norouzi, M., Xu, K., & Schaal, S. (2016). Bridging the gap between value and policy based reinforcement learning.
  - Connects different learning paradigms

## Surveys and Reviews
- Kaelbling, L. P., Littman, M. L., & Moore, A. W. (1996). Reinforcement learning: A survey.
  - Comprehensive overview of RL methods
