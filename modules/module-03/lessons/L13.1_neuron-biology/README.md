## Biological Neurons

The neuron is the basic building block of the nervous system. It receives signals from other neurons through dendrites, processes them in the cell body, and sends signals through the axon to other neurons.

Key components:
- Dendrites: Receive input signals
- Cell body (soma): Processes the inputs
- Axon: Transmits the output signal
- Synapses: Connections to other neurons

This biological inspiration led to the development of artificial neurons in computational models.
